    At this website, tropical depressions are identified by an alphabet for
    users' convenience. This alphabet is not assigned in chronological order.
    When tropical depressions have developed to tropical storms, typhoon
    numbers are assigned instead.
    Units:
    1 kt (knot) = 1.852 km/h = 0.5144 m/s
    1 NM (nautical mile) = 1.852 km

    Abbreviations:
    TY: Typhoon
    STS: Severe Tropical Storm
    TS: Tropical Storm
    TD: Tropical Depression
    LOW: Extra-tropical Low
